Privacy Policy for Colda

Effective date: March 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Colda, provided by Blabbit, handles information when you use the app and related website.

1. Overview

Colda is a price tracking and comparison app that helps users manage items, stores, and price records. We aim to keep the app simple and privacy-conscious.

2. Information You Provide

When you use Colda, you may enter information such as:

  • item names
  • folder names
  • store names
  • price records, quantities, and dates
  • app preferences such as language or display settings

This information is used to provide the core functionality of the app.

3. How Information Is Stored

At this stage, Colda primarily stores user-entered data locally on the device in order to support app features such as price comparison, history, and settings.

If cloud features, analytics, advertising, or other online services are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

4. How Information Is Used

Information entered in Colda is used for purposes such as:

  • displaying and managing items, folders, stores, and price records
  • comparing prices across stores
  • calculating unit prices
  • remembering your app settings and preferences
  • improving usability and maintaining the app

5. Data Sharing

Colda does not sell your personal information.

As of this version, the app is not intended to share user-entered price tracking data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

6. Third-Party Services

Colda may use platform or infrastructure services required for app distribution and operation, such as Apple services related to the App Store.

If additional third-party services are introduced in the future, such as analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs, this Privacy Policy may be revised to reflect those changes.

7. Data Retention

User-entered data is generally retained until it is deleted by the user or removed from the device, depending on how the app is used.

8. Children's Privacy

Colda is not specifically directed to children under the age required by applicable law. If you believe that inappropriate information has been provided through the app, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the app, legal requirements, or operational needs. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the app, please contact us through the support page.
