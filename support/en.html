Colda Support

Need help with Colda? This page provides basic support information, contact details, and answers to common questions.

Contact

If you have questions, suggestions, or trouble using the app, please contact us by email.

help.blabbit@gmail.com

Please include [Colda] in the subject line, along with your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue if possible.

Common Help

  • Use the Compare tab to view the latest price records for each store.
  • You can add price records by selecting an item, store, price, quantity, and date.
  • Items and stores can be managed from the Manage tab.
  • Sorting and display options can be adjusted in Settings.

Version Info

You can check the current app version inside the app from:

Settings → About → App Version

Response Time

We will do our best to review support requests as soon as possible. Response times may vary depending on volume and language.
